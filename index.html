WILD RICE AND MUSHROOM SOUP

I made this recipe last night and I'm obsessed! You will need an Instant Pot for this recipe. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 125g raw cashews
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 ribs of celery, diced
  • 2 small carrots, diced
  • 250g button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried sage
  • 140g wild rice blend
  • 1 litre vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 60g baby spinach
  • salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Place cashews in a small bowl. Cover with boiling water and let soak them soak while you prepare the rest of the ingredients
  2. Place remaining ingredients except soy sauce and spinach in the Instant Pot. Cook on manual, high pressure for 40 minutes
  3. Whilst the soup is cooking, drain and rinse cashews. Place in blender with 250ml of water and blend until completely smooth and creamy
  4. After the timer is up, carefully quick release any remaining steam using the valve on top. Remove the lid and turn the machine off
  5. Stir cashew milk into soup along with the soy sauce and spinach
  6. Serve with crusty bread or croutons!
